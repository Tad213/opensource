    • Ripping the game directly from the disk to your USB Loader without a computer is very helpful and what we teach very quickly in this tutorial.

    What you need

  • A modded Wii
  • A Wii game to rip
  • A Flash drive
  • USB Loader

    • How to

    Simply load up USB loader and then place the disc in the drive, it will prompt you with 3 options, select install then once it finishes hover over the blank case and click 1 then select what type of case options you want then click yes and you're done click on the game and play.
