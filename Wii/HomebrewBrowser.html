    • Description

    The Homebrew Browser allows you to download some homebrew applications without needing a computer.

    What you need

  • a modded Wii
  • A SD card
  • These files

    • How to

    To install simply unzip these files onto the root of your SD card, put it in the Wii and navigate to the Homebrew channel and click on the Homebrew Browser and wait for it to load.
