    • Description

    Flashing your drive allows you to simply place a DVD that you bought from a store for ~$2 and burned a iso for a xbox onto it and it works, without a flashed drive it will simply say that it cannot read the disk. This style is for xbox slims with a hitachi or a liteon DG-16D5S if you have any other type of drive click here.

    What you need

  • Xbox 360 Slim
  • Replacement PCB
  • you will also need a DGX which you're own your own or simply use a cr4 xl
  • and something to dump your nand you can use a DemoN* or a j-runner

    • *a DemoN can be used to dual boot if you plan to RGH or R-JTag

    How To

    Simply hook up your nand dumper and then dump your nand twice and make sure they match using j-runner then solder in your cr4 xl and load up xell to see your cpu key. Take a picture of it, then enter it into J-runner and you will have your DVD key, load it onto your replacement pcb and then simply take your old one out an hook your new one in then simply uninstall your cr4 and nand dumper unless you are planning to rgh aswell and then close up and your done, make sure you stealth patch your iso's and your good. video coming soon.
